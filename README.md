# sales-analysis-dashboard
## 📌 Objective

Analyze sales data to identify trends, top-performing products, and key business insights.

## 📊 Dataset

Superstore Sales Dataset
Contains data on orders, customers, regions, and product categories.

## ⚙️ Approach

* Data cleaning and preprocessing
* Feature engineering (Year, Month)
* Exploratory data analysis
* Data visualization

## 📈 Analysis Performed

* Sales by Category
* Sales by Region
* Sales by Segment
* Monthly Sales Trend
* Top Sub-Categories by Sales
* Top Products by Revenue

## 🔍 Key Insights

* Technology category generates the highest sales
* Phones and Chairs are top-performing sub-categories
* Consumer segment contributes the highest revenue
* West and East regions outperform others
* Sales show seasonal patterns with peak months
